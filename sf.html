San Francisco

SF is the fourth-most populous city in California. It was founded in 1776. The population is estimated of 8.6 million people living there. It's known for its popular tourist spots. The weather is cool summers, fog, and steep rolling hills. The sports teams are the SF Giants and the SF 49ers.
